About Leader Election

This page displays the Kubernetes leader election status for this introspect instance. Leader election ensures that only one instance is active at a time in a multi-replica deployment, providing high availability while preventing conflicts. The leader holds a lease that must be renewed periodically. If the leader fails, another instance will automatically take over.
